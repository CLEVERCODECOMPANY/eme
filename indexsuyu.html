ABOUT LEILA

Leila Suyu is an artist and painter from Alcala,Cagayan. She works mainly with oil, pen, watercolor and acrylic. She uses bright color and bold, thick brushstrokes to create her vibrant art pieces. She was worked in the field of design ,drawing and painting. She studied a Bachelor of Multimedia Art degree at Cagayan State University.
